Damage Warning Signs

Signs You May Need Termite Treatment

Termite problems are often discovered later than other pest issues because the activity can stay hidden inside wood, walls, and structural elements. By the time visible damage appears, the infestation may have already progressed.

Common warning signs include hollow-sounding wood, damaged trim, mud tubes, discarded wings, bubbling paint, and visible termite activity near structural materials or entry points.

If you suspect termites, acting sooner usually helps reduce damage exposure and treatment complexity.
