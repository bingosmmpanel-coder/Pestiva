Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Pestora respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and handled when you visit this website or contact Pestora by phone.

1. Information We May Collect

We may collect limited information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide during a phone call, such as your name, phone number, service needs, and general location.
  • Basic technical website usage data, such as device type, browser type, pages visited, and general traffic data.
  • Information collected through standard analytics or advertising tools, where used.

2. How Information May Be Used

Information may be used to:

  • Respond to service inquiries.
  • Provide pest control-related customer support.
  • Improve website performance, usability, and marketing effectiveness.

3. Call-Only Contact Model

This website is primarily structured as a call-only website. Pestora does not rely on public web forms as the main contact method. If you call, information shared during the conversation may be used to understand your service needs and respond appropriately.

This website may use cookies, analytics tools, or similar technologies to understand general visitor behavior, improve performance, and measure marketing effectiveness. These tools may collect limited non-sensitive technical and usage information.

Pestora does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only when reasonably necessary to operate the website, support marketing or analytics tools, comply with legal obligations, or support service-related communication.

7. Third-Party Services

This website may rely on third-party services such as website hosting, analytics providers, advertising platforms, call tracking tools, or similar service providers. Those services may process limited data according to their own policies.

8. External Links

This website may link to external websites or third-party services. Pestora is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites.

9. Children's Privacy

This website is not intended for children under 13, and Pestora does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the website.

Pestora may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
