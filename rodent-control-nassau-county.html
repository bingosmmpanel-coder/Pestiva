Early Warning Signs

Signs You Need Rodent Control

Rodent problems often start quietly. You may first notice sounds at night, droppings in cabinets or storage spaces, gnaw marks, nesting material, or signs of food contamination. Even one visible rodent can suggest more activity out of sight.

Rats and mice often move through walls, ceilings, utility gaps, kitchens, basements, garages, and storage areas. In commercial spaces, they can also create sanitation concerns that affect staff, customers, and operations.

If you suspect rodent activity, calling early usually gives you the best chance to limit spread and reduce disruption.
